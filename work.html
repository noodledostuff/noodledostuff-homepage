Virtual production

LED ICVFX and real-time stage infrastructure.

I built one of Hong Kong's early LED ICVFX stages hands-on: scouting high-resolution LED panels, checking refresh behavior, physically rigging the wall and making the stage usable for camera work.

The infrastructure covered render nodes, genlock, networking, Unreal Engine with nDisplay, hardware camera tracking, lens calibration and a custom mobile app for virtual camera control.

  • nDisplay
  • Genlock
  • Camera tracking
  • Lens calibration

Motion capture and virtual artists

Real-time performance pipelines.

For virtual artists and VTuber productions, I sync mocap data with Unreal Engine real-time rendering so on-location performances can become immersive live character experiences.

Years in event tech feed directly into the pipeline work: media servers, NDI streams, low-latency routing, virtual cameras and multi-operator control room planning.

Teach and learn

Education, AR/MR and media training.

I teach media production courses for NGOs and schools, working with students from curious kids to high-school crews on editing, DaVinci Resolve, multicam broadcasting and filmmaking techniques.

At City University of Hong Kong I also developed AR/MR solutions for chemistry teaching and supervised student projects. Teaching keeps the workflow honest: it must be explainable, repeatable and useful.
